Introduction

MARKED

MARKED is a digital magazine that dives beneath the surface of tattoo culture in East Asia, uncovering the layers of skin, stigma, and story. Once a symbol of deviance or punishment, tattoos now claim space as acts of defiance, healing, and self-definition. Spotlighting East Asian tattoo traditions and taboos, this magazine documents the voices of artists and wearers alike—those who are marked by choice, by heritage, or by history.
